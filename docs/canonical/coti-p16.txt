             Gunnery: Gunnery is a general skill in the use of weapons mounted aboard
         spacecraft. Possession of this skill entitles the character to the job-title Gunner.
         Gunnery skill allows an individual to operate any turret mounted weapons aboard a
         ship; these include Idsers, sandcasters, energy weapons (plasma and fusion guns in
         mounts), missiles, and particle accelerators. Use of this skill is governed by the
         space combat systems in Book 2 and Book 5. This skill is necessary for hiring on as
         a gunner on any ship. Untrained individuals do not have any skill in this field.
